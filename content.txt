What we do?
We conduct offline and online fundraisers targetted at improving the living standards of marginalised communities. 

Our Projects
1. WASH - Building toilets, Sanitation facilities and primary healthcare centers in underdeveloped rural areas. 35,000 toilets built so far.
2. Ann Daan - Daily programme where we provide free lunches in oldage homes, orphanages and homeless shelters. 23,45,000 meals provided.
3. ShikSha - Building primary schools and providing scholarships in rural areas. 15,000 children educated
